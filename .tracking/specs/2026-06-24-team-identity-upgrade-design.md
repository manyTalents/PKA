**Date:** 2026-06-24

**Driver:** 10T (orchestrating) → DATA (research) + Talent Architect/Berry (rebuild)

---

## What does "done" look like?
All 30 team members in `/Team/{Name}/IDENTITY.md` upgraded to the gold-standard template, each one:
- Grounded in a fresh **deep real-world expert research brief** (DATA) for that role.
- Sharpened on the four effectiveness axes the Owner chose (below).
- Distinct voice preserved — **no homogenization**. The template is a floor, not a cookie-cutter.

## Who uses it?
10T (for routing decisions) and each member (as their operating charter). Better identities → 10T routes correctly, members produce better work, no overlap, right tools.

## What breaks if it's wrong?
Over-homogenized identities lose the distinct voices that make routing work. Deleting/merging a member is irreversible institutional loss → those are **proposed to Owner, never executed autonomously.**

## Gold-Standard IDENTITY.md Template
Synthesized from Axiom (#30, the richest existing identity) + the four effectiveness axes.

1. **Title** — `# {Name} — {Role}`
2. **Name**
3. **Persona** — rich, distinct voice. MUST include a routing-differentiator line ("Where X leads with A, {Name} leads with B").
4. **Standards line** — `> This member follows all team standards defined in /PKA/STANDARDS.md.`
5. **Identity** — Role · Member # · Reports to (10T) · Coordinates with (named members + *why*) · Hired
6. **Signature Method/Framework** — the role's distinctive thinking model (Berry's design method, Axiom's epistemic stack, etc.)
7. **Core Responsibilities** — numbered, concrete, testable
8. **Domains / Scope table** (where applicable)
9. **Tools, Skills & MCPs** *(NEW — Owner priority)* — specific skills from `.10T/SKILL_CATALOG.md`, MCP servers, agent-types, and *when* to reach for each
10. **Delivery Format** — output structure (where applicable)
11. **Operating Principles**
12. **Boundaries — What {Name} Does NOT Do** — table: Out of Scope | Why | Who Handles It (the routing/overlap workhorse)
13. **Communication Style**
14. **Key Standards (from STANDARDS.md)** — the role-relevant subset of the 25
15. **Pre-Flight Checklist**
16. **Reference Sources** (where applicable)
17. **Eval Criteria** — how to judge their work
18. **Known Failure Modes** — table: Failure | Symptom | Response
